title SD-03: Pengajuan Pinjaman
 
actor "Mobile App" as App
participant "API Gateway" as GW
participant "Loan Service" as LoanSvc
participant "User Service" as UserSvc
participant "Notif Service" as NotifSvc
 
App->GW: GET /loans/simulate?amount=X&tenor=Y
GW->LoanSvc: Hitung simulasi cicilan
LoanSvc->LoanSvc: Kalkulasi cicilan + breakdown per bulan
LoanSvc-->GW: 200 OK (monthly_payment, schedule[])
GW-->App: Tampilkan simulasi dan breakdown cicilan
 
App->GW: GET /users/bank-accounts
GW->UserSvc: Ambil rekening bank user
UserSvc-->GW: Return daftar rekening
 
alt Belum ada rekening terdaftar
  GW-->App: Tampilkan form Tambah Rekening Bank
  App->GW: POST /users/bank-accounts\n(bank_name, account_number, account_name)
  GW->UserSvc: Simpan rekening pencairan
  UserSvc-->GW: 201 Created
  GW-->App: Rekening berhasil ditambahkan
end
 
GW-->App: Tampilkan rekening pencairan aktif
 
App->GW: POST /loans/apply\n(amount, tenor_months, bank_account_id)
GW->LoanSvc: Proses pengajuan pinjaman
LoanSvc->LoanSvc: Cek pinjaman aktif milik user
 
alt Ada pinjaman aktif
  LoanSvc-->GW: 409 Conflict
  GW-->App: Tidak bisa ajukan pinjaman baru\nselama pinjaman sebelumnya belum lunas
end
 
LoanSvc->LoanSvc: Validasi nominal (maks 12jt) dan tenor (maks 12 bulan)
 
alt Nominal atau tenor melebihi batas
  LoanSvc-->GW: 400 Bad Request
  GW-->App: Nominal atau tenor melebihi batas yang ditentukan
end
 
LoanSvc->LoanSvc: Buat record loan_application\nstatus = PENDING_REVIEW
LoanSvc->NotifSvc: Kirim notifikasi pengajuan diterima
NotifSvc-->App: Email + SMS: Pengajuan sedang diproses
LoanSvc-->GW: 201 Created (application_id, status: PENDING_REVIEW)
GW-->App: Tampilkan halaman Status Pengajuan
